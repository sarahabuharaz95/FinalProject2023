Janae Price is a video producer who specializes in hosting and making content for Thrillist socials like Tik Tok and Instagram.

Janae believes that telling a story can be done through any medium be it written or video, editorial or social. For the past three years, Janae has written, hosted, and produced a number of projects, from creating and hosting her own podcast, Sufficiently Black, to working in social media. For Thrillist, Janae has both audio and on-camera experience with organic content like the History of podcast and Ride with Me. Janae also has experience hosting branded and sponsored content under the Thrillist Explores franchise. And for her day-to-day you can find her hosting and producing a few different series on the Thrillist Tik Tok from How to Make Friends to her green screen series.

Originally from upstate New York, Janae has a lengthy editorial background with a bachelor's degree in journalism from Hofstra University. In her free time she loves podcasting, anime, k-pop, and eating anything covered in cheese.
